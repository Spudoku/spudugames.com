About

What is Spudugames?

This is a place where I get to talk about things and show off my game development "expertise". Basically it's my own playground on the internet where I do whatever, whenever.

Who are you?

I go by several aliases, but you can call me Spuduking (Spoo-doo-king). I like to garden, read, hike, play games and make games. I'm currently studying Computer Science.

The Blogs

I might attempt to stick to some kind of schedule when adding blog entries.

Game Discussions

These will be the bread-and-butter of the blog section of this website. They include reviews of specific games ("formal" and "informal"), interesting game mechanics and anything else that I find interesting about games, their development and their execution.

Author Updates

These blog entries will be more personal, such as cool things I've learned, what I've been doing IRL, or why x-y-z project is being delayed.

Brain Leaks

Whatever I want to post that doesn't fit into the above categories. This might include hot takes, "shower thoughts", art, poetry, creative writing, and so on. Don't expect everything to be SFW, or "polite". In other words, Brain Leaks are not appropriate for children, nor for adults who don't act like children.
